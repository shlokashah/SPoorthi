What We Have For You

SPoorthi is the sports fest organized by Sardar Patel Institute Of Technology which brings together thousands of individuals across Mumbai and other cities to compete amongst each other and be the best. The series of sports events organized at SPoorthi aim at challenging the skills of an individual thus boosting it and mainly keeping the sportsmanspirit within us ALIVE. The fire within should never burn out is what is the motto of this fest.

SPIT Marathon is the most sensational sport event of the college. It's a 5km long marathon starting from Juhu beach till our College.
